From Chicago
With Love

We shot five Chicago-based artists at one of the most diversely curated spots in the city, Salvage One. A vibrant and endless showroom filled with recycled art, furniture and relics of the past.

Each creative woman in front of the camera claimed their own unique space in a sea of color and design.

Filling this three-story wonderland with that singular Chicago reverb you just know - if you know.

We got to know more about the creative process behind each artist, what makes them tick, and what moves them to do what they do.
